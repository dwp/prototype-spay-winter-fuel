You do not qualify for a Winter Fuel Payment

For winter 2024, we worked out your payment during the week of 16 to 22 September. This is called "qualifying week."

You do not qualify because you were not receiving Pension Credit, a means-tested benefit or tax credit during this week.

You can check online if you can apply for Pension Credit. Search GOV.UK for "Pension Credit". Or you can call 0800 99 1234.
